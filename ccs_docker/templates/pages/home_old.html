Information At Your Fingertips


As a citizen, you can do the following without logging in:

  1. Search for the involvement of any vehicle in crimes in West Bengal against a database of {{ vehicle_count }} vehicles by visiting Vehicle Search
  2. Locate any police station in West Bengal on the map, and get information about it by visiting Police Stations

For authorized police-officers, it is a web-accessible database of {{ crime_count }} records of crimes, {{ criminal_count }} criminals, and {{ involvement_count }} linkages between the two.

This application was created for the use of police officers of West Bengal by Shri Sanjay Singh, IPS, (WB:1992), and went online in August, 2015.

It was nominated for DG, NCRB's trophy for in-house IT development in the same year, and was shortlisted in the 5-best applications from amongst 47 entries sent by 19 police organisations across the country. It received high commendation from DG, NCRB.



The project, and its adoption by all police-officers of West Bengal, was made possible due to the support of the following officers:
